What Is a PC?

A computer is an object that can be given an input and create an output, similarly the brain can be considered a computer as well, and scientists continue to discover more impressive features of the brain and how it works. But in most cases the word “Computer” is to talk about an “electronic device containing a microprocessor”.

What is a Microprocessor? A Microprocessor is a small electronic device that can process any data given instantaneously. They can be commonly found in day to day devices such as PCs, Vehicles, TVs, Phones, etc.

PC’s were officially stamped as a sought after home appliance after the Apple II was created, although the Altair 8800 was the first “Personal Computer”. The Apple II proved that there could be a demand for personal computers because they could be used in homes and at school.

PCs must include these parts to function:

  • Motherboards
  • Power Supplies
  • CPUs
  • Ram
  • Drives
  • Cooling
  • Cables

All of these components work together in order to make a PC fully functional in order to actually give it inputs and receive outputs.

Powering up a PC

When you first press the power button on the case of the PC several internal processes start before you can use your PC, this is called the “Boot Process”. During this process, the power button starts your power supply giving your entire PC power. This process is controlled by your basic input output system or “BIOS” for short and stored on a flash memory chip but in a pc It’s embedded on the motherboard. The Bios also provides a basic menu to check things like drives during the boot process.

Computer Hardware:

What is computer hardware? Computer hardware, simply means “any physical parts of components that contribute to a computer system”. There are many different types of computer hardware inside both Desktop PC’s and Laptop PC’s (Laptop hardware is generally smaller so it can fit in a compact design).

Different Between “Software” and “Hardware”:

Hardware is something you are able to physically touch and put together in order for a computer to work, and function(Inputs and outputs). To be considered hardware it must be tangible and for example, be screwed into a PC case.

Software: Software, similarly, is something that pretty much makes a PC do what it can do, but it is NOT physical, so you cannot actually touch it, but it could be programs that you install on your PC to create more practicality. It could be defined as “A set of instructions to tell you're PC how to work”.

Different types of Hardwares

Motherboards are the hub of your computer. It connects to all the other pieces of hardware and allows them to function by sending and controlling how much power is getting sent to each hardware for them to properly operate.

The Central processing unit or CPU is your processing power for running your computer. It’s the brain of the computer, It’s responsible for processing the data your computer needs to process and operate.

Components of a robot:

The control system is all the components that make up the robot's central processing unit. It’s programmed to tell the robot how to utilize specific components just like the human brain. Sensors provide the robot with electrical signals that are processed by the and allow the robot to interact with the outside world. Common sensors include video cameras, photoresistors, and microphones, this allows the robot to process information about its surroundings and utilize it to make decisions. Actuators are responsible for giving the robot its ability to move its limbs around. They receive the electrical signals from the robot’s central processing unit and move to carry out the necessary actions. The robot also needs a power source to function which is most commonly a lead acid battery for its safe qualities and long shelf life, but there are also the more expensive silver cadmium batteries that can be used. End effectors are the tools the robot needs in order to perform its job. They can be screwdriver heads, paintbrushes, or even a claw.

What is Robotics:

Robotics is the injection of science, engineering, and technology that contribute to the production of certain machines, known as robots. They replicate or substitute human actions, and can perform basic and repetitive tasks with a greater efficiency and accuracy than humans, which makes them the ideal tool for industries such as manufacturing. However, the introduction of artificial intelligence in robotics has given robots the ability to handle more and more complex situations in various industries as well.

What is a Robot:

A robot is a programmable machine that can complete a specific task. The term robotics describes the field of study in which describes the development of a robot, or automation. Each robot has a different level of autonomy (how automatic it is) and there are level ranges on how much a human will need to interact with it to function.

What are the main components of a Robot:

  • End Effectors

What is it:

A blockchain is a distributed database that is shared among a huge number of computer networks. It is best known for its key role in trading and selling cryptocurrencies, and maintaining records of transactions of every cryptocurrency exchange. There is no way to revert any history in the blockchain, therefore it is extremely reliable when trying to exchange with other people, therefore it is a literal block of information, unable to be altered by any third party source.

How it works:

A blockchain is similar to a database with a key difference to how the data is stored and accessed. It uses a script to enter and access information as well as store it elsewhere. The blockchain then collects the information and makes it into a hexadecimal number called a hash. The hash is then entered into the following block header and encrypted with the other information. What is the IoT:

The Internet of Things is the network of physical “things' that are embedded into sensors, software, and other technologies for connecting and exchanging data over the internet. These devices can range from ordinary household objects to sophisticated industrial terms.

Why is the IoT so important?

The IoT has become one of the most important pieces of technology in the modern world. Since the creation of the IoT, we can now connect to almost every object we use in our day-to-day lives. Some examples of this are A majority of kitchen appliances, Cars, Baby Monitors, Thermostats, etc. We can also have access to the internet via embedded devices, which can come with flawless communication, which becomes possible between people, processes, and things.

What technologies have made IoT possible?

A few advances in several different technologies have made it possible for IoT to exist. Those technologies bring access to low-cost, low-power sensor technology making it possible for more manufacturers. Connectivity is through a “host” that allows all IoT technologies to transfer data efficiently. Cloud computing platforms allow both businesses and consumers to access the infrastructure they need to scale up without having to manage everything. Machine learning allows the IoT to access a cloud of information from the past and other robots for them to adapt to different needs.

How is the IoT applicable in our lives?

The IoT has changed our lives through increased conveniences of everyday tasks, which include home security systems, wearable devices to improve health and wellness, and improved connectivity.
